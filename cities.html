📖 Methodology

Camera counts are queried from OpenStreetMap via the Overpass API. We query for nodes tagged surveillance:type=ALPR (automated license plate readers) and man_made=surveillance within each city's bounding box.

Density is calculated as total cameras ÷ bounding box area (km²). Cities with larger footprints naturally have lower densities even if they have more cameras. The bounding boxes are conservative approximations of each city's metropolitan area.

Limitations: OSM coverage varies significantly by city — denser mapping communities may show higher counts. Zero values indicate either very low coverage or API rate-limit failures at query time. This data is for informational and research purposes only.
